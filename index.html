What is Scandocs?

Scandocs is a free document scanner provided by Techkets. You can easily manage your documents and create extremely high-quality PDFs with just one click.

Use your mobile and generate a pdf for your photos and documents in no time. With Scandocs you will experience the world-class user experience with a very decent, eye-catching, and extremely researched UI.

How Scandocs work?

Create your documents by importing images from the gallery, or use the Scandocs in-built camera for capturing multiple images at once. Our camera supports clicking images in batch mode, after importing all of your images just wait for a little until all the images gets imported. Once done you can enhance your images using the crop tool, or by applying some cool and helpful filters on it. When you get satisfied with all the images just click on the download button to download the PDF, or you can also share the PDF directly.
