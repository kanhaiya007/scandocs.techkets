What is Techkets?

Techkets is an open-source organization where people with ideas build something innovative. We are open to all and, we will provide you the best team for your idea. If you want to learn something or if you want to work on something just contact us.
We are growing every single day and making our organization the best open-source organization. We do not charge anything from anyone. So in the future, if you have any idea to work on but no money, remember we are with you.
